What We Offer

Everything You Need to
Stay Safe on the Road

A complete toolkit for community-driven road safety — from reporting to real-time alerts and analytics.

Real-Time Hazard Reporting
Report potholes, accidents, waterlogging, and debris in seconds. Geotagged and verified by nearby users instantly.
Smart Proximity Alerts
Get push notifications when a verified hazard is ahead on your route. Our AI predicts risks before you encounter them.
AI-Powered Analysis
Machine learning models identify patterns, predict high-risk zones, and automatically prioritize critical alerts.
Community Verification
Reports gain credibility through upvotes. The more people confirm, the higher the alert level — no false alarms.
Analytics Dashboard
Detailed heatmaps and trend data for city planners, traffic authorities, and road maintenance agencies.
Driving Simulator
Test and train hazard recognition skills in our interactive driving simulator — gamified safety education.
The Process

How It Works

From report to resolution in four simple steps — powered by you.

01
Spot & Report
See a hazard? Tap report. Add a photo, tag the type, and submit in under 10 seconds.
02
Community Validates
Nearby users confirm the hazard. Multiple confirmations boost the alert level automatically.
03
Alert Broadcast
Drivers on the affected route receive real-time push alerts with distance and severity info.
04
Resolution Tracked
Authorities are notified. Once fixed, the community marks it resolved and the alert clears.
